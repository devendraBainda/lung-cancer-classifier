About This Classifier

This tool uses deep learning to classify lung tissue images into three categories:

  • Lung Benign Tissue: Normal, non-cancerous lung tissue
  • Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma: A type of non-small cell lung cancer
  • Lung Adenocarcinoma: Another type of non-small cell lung cancer

For accurate results, please upload clear, high-quality images of lung tissue samples.
